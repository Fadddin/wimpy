---
title: "I remember fragments of my past but i am not sure if i ever lived them"
description: "reflection on memory, identity."
date: "2024-01-25"
---


I remember fragments of my past,  
like scattered glass carried on the wind,  
I wonder if I ever truly lived them,  
or if they were dreams that dissolved with the dawn.  

Memories move like shadows on water,  
familiar, yet distant, slipping through my fingers,  
as if I touched the echo 
that might have been mine but never was.
